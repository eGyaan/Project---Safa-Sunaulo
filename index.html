Introduction

Safa Sunaulo Nepal (SSN), a non-profit social organization registered at Kathmandu District Administration Office (DAO) on 2070 B.S is dedicated to help the people living in diverse communities throughout the country. For this, they have local action taken by every community and volunteers that are motivated to provide their services where need arise. The activities undertaken will mainly focus in improving the environment in localities by focusing in providing and conserving water and undertaking environmental awareness where issues regarding healthcare will be one of their main themes. SSN will join hands with local authorities, agencies and the international community to make the health and well-being of the people living in such surroundings especially saving lives of infants and new born. Nepal still lacks success in reducing infant mortality rates as per the MDG plan. By using simple, low-cost training methods to bring infant mortality rate low is of utmost importance of SSN to make the lives healthy for newborn and SSN will be actively involved in reducing infant mortality rates, at least initially by starting the Helping Baby Breath program in Nepal and also various nutrition programs.
